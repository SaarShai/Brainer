## Falsification evidence (new negatives are not tautological)

- Pre-fix falsification (lane A): all 100 C600 notification cases FIRED on the
  G1 hook before the fix was written — the negatives detect the old defect.
- Legacy control: the old form false-fires on 50/50 C600 and 25/25 C675
  notification negatives — the corpus can detect a broken rule.
- Live-shape end-to-end: the exact live-observed notification strings
  (multiline, entity-escaped) suppress under G2+; a real dynamic-workflow
  subagent completion still fires.
- Adversarial (2026-07-18 audit): 7 novel auditor-authored fault shapes were
  predicted and empirically CONFIRMED to fail under G3 — proving the corpus
  method finds real holes when authorship is independent. These shapes become
  C850-class cases in G4 (lane A3), keeping the adversarial provenance.

## Honest limits

1. Frozen-corpus precision does not transfer to live precision (G1 proved it:
   1.000 frozen, 0.70–0.82 live). The preregistered 95% gate is a LIVE gate;
   G3/G4 have not yet faced live traffic. The long-horizon preregistration
   (`eval/skills_effectiveness/longhorizon_preregistration_draft.md`) is the
   decision instrument.
2. C600/C675 were authored by the same round that authored G2/G3 (mitigated by
   pre-fix falsification + legacy control); the C850-class cases are the first
   adversary-authored generation.
3. G3 carries known CRITICAL defects found by the audit (provenance bypass,
   flood-destroyed deferred fire, reconciliation errors) — fixed in G4 before
   any live exposure per the preregistration's pre-live blockers.
